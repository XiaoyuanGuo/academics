About

I am currently a Software Engineer at Mineral.ai (an Alphabet company), where I conduct R&D in zero/few-shot object detection, multi-modality learning, and foundation models. I obtained my PhD from Emory University.

My expertise lies at the intersection of Computer Vision and Medical Image Processing. My previous work involved deep learning for medical analysis with collaborators at Emory and Georgia State University.
